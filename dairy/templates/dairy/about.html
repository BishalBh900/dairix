💡 What is Dairix?
Dairix is a full-featured dairy management platform designed to simplify and digitise daily operations for milk collection centers and co-operatives. From farmer onboarding to automated billing and financial reports — everything is in one place, accessible from any device.
🌿 Key Features
  • 🥛
    Morning & evening milk collection with fat and SNF tracking
  • 👨‍🌾
    Farmer management with individual profiles and ledgers
  • 🧾
    Automated billing and payment records
  • 📊
    Analytics, reports, and daily breakdowns
  • 🛒
    Purchase register for tracking cooperative expenses
  • 📢
    Notice board with role-based visibility
  • 🔒
    Role-based access — Admin, Staff, and Farmer views
  • 📱
    Fully responsive — works on mobile, tablet, and desktop
